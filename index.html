About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Intelligent Secure Systems IRC, King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals (KFUPM). With a Ph.D. in Communication Engineering from National Central University, my research focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence, vehicular networks, and next-generation communication systems. I specialize in developing innovative solutions for V2X networks, satellite-aided vehicular communications, and multi-agent reinforcement learning systems that enhance connectivity and spectrum management in Beyond 5G networks.
