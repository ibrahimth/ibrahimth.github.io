  • Electronic Sabic Dictionary

    The Electronic Sabic Dictionary is a comprehensive tool that translates Sabic into Arabic, English, and French. It incorporates the ancient South Arabian alphabet (also known as musnad) which branched from the Proto-Sinaitic alphabet in about the 9th century BC. The dictionary includes both the monumental Musnad script and the cursive Zabur script used by the ancient Yemenis.
